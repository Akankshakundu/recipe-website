Pasta 🍝

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Pasta(penne/fusilli/spaghetti)- 1 cup
  • White sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil- 1tbsp
  • Salt- 1tsp
  • Water- 4-5 cups

Vegetables

  • Capsicum
  • Sweet Corn
  • Mushroom
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
    • Take a large pan ,add water,salt, and a little oil
    • Bring it to boil
    • Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes(until soft but not mushy)
    • Drain and keep aside
  2. Add flavors
    • Add salt, black pepper, oregano, chilli flakes
    • Add cheese and mix well
  3. Add veggies
    • Toss veggies in the sauce and cook for 2–3 minutes
  4. Mix pasta
    • Add boiled pasta into the sauce
    • Mix everything nicely
    • Cook for 1–2 minutes
  5. 🍽️ Final Touch
    • Garnish with extra cheese or herbs
    • Serve hot 😋
