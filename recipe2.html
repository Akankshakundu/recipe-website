Pizza 🍕

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  1. For Dough
    • Maida (all-purpose flour) – 2 cups
    • Dry yeast – 1 tsp
    • Sugar – 1 tsp
    • Salt – ½ tsp
    • Warm water – ~¾ cup
    • Oil – 1 tbsp
  2. For Toppings
    • Pizza sauce (or tomato ketchup) – 3–4 tbsp
    • Cheese (mozzarella preferred) – 1 cup
    • Onion (sliced)
    • Capsicum
    • Sweet corn
    • Olives (optional)
    • Chili flakes & oregano

      • Steps

      1. Activate Yeast
        • Take warm water (not hot)
        • Add sugar + yeast
        • Mix and let it sit for 5–10 minutes
        • 👉 It should become frothy (this means yeast is active)
      2. Make Dough
        • In a bowl, mix maida + salt
        • Add yeast mixture + oil
        • Knead into a soft dough (8–10 minutes)
        • Cover and let it rest for 1–2 hours (it will double in size)
      3. Prepare Base
        • Punch down the dough
        • Roll it into a circle (thick or thin as you like)
        • Place on a baking tray
        • Prick with a fork (prevents puffing)
      4. Add Toppings
        • Spread pizza sauce
        • Add cheese
        • Add veggies
        • Sprinkle oregano & chili flakes
      5. Bake Pizza
        • Preheat oven at 200°C (10 minutes)
        • Bake pizza for 12–15 minutes until cheese melts and crust turns golden
      6. 🍽️ Final Result
        • Slice and serve hot
        • Enjoy that cheesy stretch 😋
